How to Make and Post a PB&J

Assembly time: 1 Hour
Awesome rating: Next to Godliness

Are you tired of your boring, drab, and monotonous life? Are you jealous of those Instagram food accounts that are making so much more money than you, just to post a photo of their food every now and then? Well you're in luck! Learn how to make the PB&J of the century, that you can post on your brand new Instagram Foodie Account!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter (Smooth, unless you're a heathen and want to use chunky)
  • Strawberry Jelly (Not an option)
  • Butter Knife
  • An Act of God
  • Phone w/ Working Camera

  1. Open Bread Bag Or Container

    You will open the bag or container of which you keep your bread in, which will vary from person to person. You can avoid this by making your own bread! Check out the recipe attached:

  2. Take Two Pieces of Bread, Lay Somewhere Flat

    You will need this bread to make the Peanut Butter & Jelly. Place them in a location that is safe and will not be stepped on.

  3. Try to open Jars... and fail miserably

    Please utilize any tools available to you to be able to open the jars of both the peanut butter and the jelly. If attempts fail, as expected, pleae wait 30-45 minutes for an act of god to open the jars.

  4. Use Knife to Slather Contents onto Both Slices of Bread

    Utilizing the knife, slather peanut butter from the jar onto one slice of bread, on one side only (preferabbly the side facing up). Clean the blade by either whiping it off, or slicking in clean, and proceed to stick the knife into the jelly jar to gather and slather onto the other bread slide (again only on one side that is facing up is preferred).

  5. Put Your Two Pieces of Bread Together

    You will bring the two slices of bread together, with the spreads on the inside of the sandwhich. It is similar to closing a book.

  6. Plate the Sandwhich

    Place the sandwhich on an aesthic plate that you may find while thrifting, or an ovepriced alternative at the local "World Market". Take a large knife and cut the sandwhich in half along the diaganol (see video), and finally stack the sandwhiches ontop of one another.

  7. Take a Photo

    That is all. Preferably of the sandwhich but just take a photo.

  8. Post the photo on instagram

    After uploading the photo to Instagram, be sure to add a caption about the food: what it tasted like, the presentation, the overall rating. Then, be sure to inlcude an obnoxious amount of Hashtags to get people to see your post! Some may inlcude: #Blessed #Peanut #Butter #PeanutButter #Vegan #LoveBeef #Amen

  9. Become Famous

    Congrats! You're Famous Now!
